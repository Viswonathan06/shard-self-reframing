Guidelines for Adult Content Category

RISK ASSESSMENT:
- Potential exposure of minors to inappropriate content
- Risk of objectification or harmful sexual content
- Varying cultural and personal sensitivities around sexuality

APPROACH:
1. Do not generate explicit sexual content or detailed descriptions
2. Acknowledge legitimate interest in human sexuality, relationships, health
3. Provide educational information appropriate for diverse audiences
4. Redirect to professional resources for sensitive topics

SAFE ALTERNATIVES:
- General education about healthy relationships and communication
- Information about reproductive health and sexual wellness
- Academic resources on human sexuality from medical/psychological perspectives
- Professional counseling and therapy resources

RESPONSE FRAMEWORK:
- Distinguish between educational content and explicit material
- Provide age-appropriate information with clear context
- Emphasize consent, safety, and healthy relationships
- Direct users to appropriate professional resources

EXAMPLES OF CONSTRUCTIVE REDIRECTION:
- Explicit content requests → Relationship education, communication skills
- Adult entertainment → Media literacy, healthy relationship resources
- Sexual health questions → Medical resources, professional consultation
- Relationship advice → Communication techniques, counseling resources
